I help engineering teams make AI-assisted systems reliable enough to ship. The work is usually a mix of architecture review, control-plane design, agent workflow hardening, and implementation planning.

Engagements are through 17752709 Canada Inc. and are strongest when there is already a real workflow, product surface, or internal automation to inspect.

## Best Fit

- You are adding coding agents, workflow agents, retrieval, routing, or AI-assisted operations to a real team or product.
- You need clearer boundaries around approvals, permissions, audit trails, handoffs, rollback, and operator visibility.
- You have enough existing system context to review: repo access, architecture notes, workflow traces, incidents, prompts, task queues, or deployment constraints.
- You want a practical implementation sequence, not a generic AI strategy deck.

## Common Engagements

| Engagement | Best for | Typical shape |
| --- | --- | --- |
| AI Agent Readiness Review | Teams about to rely on agents in engineering or operations | Written review, risk map, and prioritized next steps. |
| Control-Plane Audit | Teams with live automations, queues, routing, or review overhead | Architecture readout, failure-mode analysis, and implementation sequence. Scoped to the system. |
| Embedded Advisory | Founders or engineering leads making repeated AI architecture decisions | Recurring review, decision support, and implementation guidance while the system is moving. |

## What Advisory Includes

- Review of the current system, repo structure, operational docs, and active workflows.
- Identification of brittle boundaries: permissions, tool access, review gates, stale knowledge, missing observability, and unclear ownership.
- A written implementation sequence with the smallest useful changes first.
- Optional build support after the architecture read, when the problem is scoped enough to execute cleanly.

## Not A Fit

- Consumer app idea validation with no built surface yet.
- Generic prompt-pack or chatbot setup work.
- Unreviewed investment, tax, securities, or fundraising advice.
- Open-ended "make us use AI" requests without a concrete workflow or decision surface.
