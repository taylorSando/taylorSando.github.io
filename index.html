Background

I've been a self-directed student of mathematics — abstract algebra, differential geometry, real analysis — since 2015. I went through a long Austrian economics phase (Mises, Hayek, Peter Schiff) that I've mostly outgrown but don't regret. I maintain a long-running stream-of-consciousness knowledge base covering everything from epistemology and social cognition to category theory and event-driven architecture — an ongoing experiment in making thinking visible and queryable.

I believe that writing is how you find out what you actually think, and that the gap between knowing and doing is where most interesting problems live.
